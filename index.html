Carrots

Carrots are a root vegetable that is orange in color. They are a good source of vitamins A, C, and K, as well as fiber. Carrots can be eaten raw, cooked, or juiced.

Health benefits of carrots

  • Improve vision
  • Boost the immune system
  • Reduce the risk of heart disease
  • Improve digestion
  • Protect against cancer

Ways to eat carrots

  • Raw
  • Cooked
  • Juiced
  • Added to soups and stews
  • Used in salads
